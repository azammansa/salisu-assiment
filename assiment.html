yummys spot menu

Appetizers

  • Caprese Skewers: Fresh mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, and basil leaves threaded onto skewers, drizzled with balsamic glaze.
  • Spinach and Artichoke Dip: Creamy blend of spinach, artichokes, and melted cheese served with tortilla chips for dipping.
  • Stuffed Mushrooms: Mushrooms filled with a mixture of breadcrumbs, garlic, herbs, and cream cheese, baked until golden brown.

Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon with Lemon-Dill Sauce: Succulent grilled salmon fillets topped with a zesty lemon-dill sauce, served with steamed asparagus.
  • Chicken Alfredo Pasta: Tender chicken breast strips tossed with fettuccine pasta and creamy Alfredo sauce, garnished with parsley.
  • Vegetable Stir-Fry: Colorful medley of stir-fried broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, and snap peas served over a bed of jasmine rice.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Lava Cake: Rich and gooey chocolate cake with a molten center, served warm and dusted with powdered sugar.
  • Strawberry Cheesecake: Creamy cheesecake with a graham cracker crust, topped with fresh strawberries and a strawberry glaze.
  • Mango Sorbet: Refreshing and fruity mango sorbet, served in a chilled bowl and garnished with mint leaves.
